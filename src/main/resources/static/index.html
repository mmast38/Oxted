About Oxted

Oxted is a picturesque village known for its rich history, stunning countryside, and welcoming community. Whether you're exploring its historic landmarks or enjoying the serene landscapes, Oxted offers something for everyone.

Local Attractions

Master Park - A beautiful green space in the center of the village, perfect for picnics and leisurely strolls.

Oxted Mill - A historic landmark with fascinating heritage, offering a glimpse into the village's past.

North Downs - Stunning walks and breathtaking views, ideal for nature lovers and hikers.
